Passion Meets Expertise

Mark Cox began his fitness journey over 15 years ago with a simple goal: to help people unlock their potential. What started as a passion quickly became a calling.

Based in Amsterdam, Mark has worked with over 500 clients — from beginners taking their first steps to elite athletes pushing their limits. His approach combines cutting-edge sports science with genuine care for each individual's journey.

Mark believes that fitness isn't just about physical transformation — it's about building confidence, discipline, and a lifestyle that supports your biggest goals.
